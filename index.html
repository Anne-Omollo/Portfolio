About Me

I am a Geospatial Analyst with a strong background in GIS, remote sensing, and data analysis. I specialize in leveraging geospatial technology to address real-world challenges in agriculture, public health, and environmental management. With expertise in tools like QGIS, Python, and Google Earth Engine, I bring data to life through interactive maps and dashboards.

Projects

Tools: Sentinel-5P, GEE, QGIS, Excel

Crop health analysis

Tools: Sentinel2_SR, GEE, QGIS, Excel

Cholera Risk Analysis

Tools: QGIS, Python, Excel
